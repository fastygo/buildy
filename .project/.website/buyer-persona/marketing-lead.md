# Buyer Persona: Marketing Lead

## Profile

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| Title | Head of Marketing, Growth Lead, Demand Gen Director, Content Lead |
| Company | SaaS startup, scale-up, or agency client team |
| Reports to | CMO, founder, or VP Marketing |
| Tools today | HubSpot, Webflow/WordPress, Figma, GA, campaign spreadsheets |
| BuildY role | Champion for landing velocity; influences buy with founder/CMO |

## Goals

- Launch and iterate **campaign landings** without engineering queue.
- Keep **brand and messaging consistent** while testing variants.
- Hand engineering **structured pages**, not one-off HTML emergencies.
- Enable content team to update copy, SEO, and blocks after go-live.

## Pain points

- Engineering backlog measured in weeks for "simple" landing changes.
- Webflow/CMS silos don't connect to product app or admin story.
- AI-generated pages are **hardcoded** — marketing still blocked post-launch.
- Agency builds are expensive; internal tools don't match design system.

## BuildY value proposition

> "Marketing-owned content on CMS-backed landings. Prototype in BuildY, publish through AppCMS — engineering only for product depth, not every headline."

## Decision criteria

1. Block-level editability (hero, pricing, FAQ, testimonials, CTA)  
2. SEO/settings fields in content model  
3. Preview before publish  
4. No developer needed for routine copy updates  
5. Visual quality competitive with agency output  

## Triggers to buy

- Product launch or repositioning with tight deadline  
- Migration off legacy CMS with broken workflow  
- Founder mandates "one stack" including marketing site  

## MVP-aligned use cases

- SaaS landing template (hero → features → pricing → social proof → FAQ → CTA)  
- Locale-ready copy (EN/RU fixture pattern mirrors BuildY app)  
- Theme and section variants for A/B narrative tests (preview-first)  

## Onboarding emphasis

Stress **ship faster** and **on-brand landings** with explicit **field-level CMS** — not chat-to-publish without structure.
