# ICP: Founder-Led SaaS Startup

## Snapshot

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Company type | B2B or B2C SaaS, pre-seed to seed |
| Team size | 1–8 people (often 0–2 engineers) |
| Geography | Global; English-first product |
| Budget | Self-serve or small team plan; price-sensitive early |
| Technical maturity | Non-technical founder or one full-stack generalist |

## Company profile

A founder-led startup preparing to launch or relaunch a product. They have a clear value proposition, early waitlist or beta users, and pressure to show a **credible product surface** — not a Carrd page with fake screenshots.

They may have tried generic AI builders and hit a wall: pretty UI, hardcoded content, no admin, no path for a future engineer to extend the codebase cleanly.

## Jobs to be done

1. Ship a **professional SaaS landing** (hero, features, pricing, FAQ, testimonials, CTA) in days.
2. Prove the product exists to investors, design partners, and early customers.
3. Keep **content editable** without redeploying for every headline change.
4. Hand a **real repository** to the first engineering hire or contractor.

## Why BuildY wins

- First proof matches their urgent need: **CMS-backed landing** with block-level structure.
- Output is **Go/Templ/AppCMS** — a serious stack story for technical due diligence.
- Preview shows **real BFF behavior**, not a video mockup.
- Export to GitHub establishes **source ownership** from day one.

## Why BuildY loses

- Founder wants zero code forever and one-click `.com` hosting only.
- Product is mobile-native or needs complex realtime social graph (out of MVP scope).
- They require React ecosystem hiring pool exclusively.

## Typical buying committee

| Role | Involvement |
|------|-------------|
| Founder / CEO | Buyer, primary end user |
| First engineer (fractional) | Evaluator, handoff recipient |
| Advisor | Influencer on stack credibility |

## Success metrics

- Time from idea to previewable landing < 1 week  
- Landing content updated via CMS without developer  
- Exported repo cloned and extended in IDE  
- AppCMS admin usable by non-developer  

## Market framing

Primary use case: **founder-led SaaS landing** — messaging emphasizes **CMS + export + FastyGo**, not "AI replaces engineering forever."
